Frequently Asked Questions

  • What should we wear?

    Cocktail attire is preferred. Boulder is warm but dry in the summer, and the ceremony venue will be shaded.

  • Are kids welcome?

    We have decided to host a wedding gathering for guests aged 18 and older. With love and respect, we ask that our guests with children make alternate arrangements.

  • Is there transportation from the airport?

    Denver International Airport (DEN) is about an hour from Boulder. If you rent a car, the St. Julien has paid self and valet parking. Rideshares are $100+ each way, or there is a bus from the airport to Downtown Boulder Station, which is a short walk from the hotel.

  • How do we get around the area?

    Boulder is a compact city and very walkable. There is also an excellent bus system, and, of course, ample parking day or night.

  • What else is cool in Colorado?

    If this is your first time in Colorado, you're in for a treat! Rocky Mountain National Park is a beautiful day trip from Boulder, and is in fact where we got engaged. Locally, there are dozens of hiking and climbing spots in and around Boulder, and Denver has a bustling downtown with museums, gardens, and stadiums. The University of Colorado at Boulder, Albert's alma mater, is known for its beautiful campus and architecture.
